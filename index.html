We accept PayPal, credit/debit cards, and cryptocurrency payments. All payments are processed securely through our client area.

Yes! All SkullHost services include enterprise-grade Layer 3 and Layer 7 DDoS protection at no extra cost. Our mitigation systems automatically detect and neutralize attacks in real time.
Can I upgrade my plan later? ▼
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from the client area. Upgrades are applied instantly with prorated billing for the remaining period.
Where are your datacenters located? ▼
SkullHost operates datacenters in Frankfurt, Germany and Dubai, UAE. Both locations feature Tier III+ infrastructure with redundant power, cooling, and network connectivity.
Do you offer refunds? ▼
We offer a 48-hour money-back guarantee on most services. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team within 48 hours of purchase for a full refund.
